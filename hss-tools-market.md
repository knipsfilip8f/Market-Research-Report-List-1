<p><p>HSS (High-Speed Steel) tools are categorized into several types based on their applications in metalworking. Milling tools are used for shaping and machining surfaces, while drilling tools are designed to create holes. Tapping tools facilitate the cutting of internal threads, and reaming & counterboring tools refine existing holes for precision. Broaching tools remove material to achieve specific shapes and features. Other HSS tools encompass a variety of specialized cutting and shaping tools, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
