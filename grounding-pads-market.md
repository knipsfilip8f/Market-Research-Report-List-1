<p><p>Grounding pads in the market primarily fall into two types: monopolar and bipolar. Monopolar grounding pads consist of a single conductive surface that connects to the ground point, often used for simpler applications where direct grounding is needed. In contrast, bipolar grounding pads feature two conductive surfaces, enabling them to create a balanced electrical field. This design is particularly beneficial for reducing interference in complex systems and enhancing overall effectiveness in grounding applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
