<p><p>The polyethylene pipes market consists of three main types: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE pipes, known for their high strength and durability, are widely used in water supply and gas distribution systems. MDPE pipes, more flexible than HDPE, are suitable for moderate pressure applications like gas and water distribution. LDPE pipes are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for low-pressure applications, such as irrigation and drainage. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
