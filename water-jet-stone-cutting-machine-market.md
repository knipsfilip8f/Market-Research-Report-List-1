<p><p>The Water-jet Stone Cutting Machine market is primarily divided into two types: Abrasive Water-jet and Pure Water-jet. Abrasive Water-jet cutting uses a mixture of water and abrasive materials to efficiently cut through hard materials like stone, providing precise and intricate cuts. In contrast, Pure Water-jet cutting utilizes only high-pressure water for softer materials or delicate applications, offering a clean cut without the risk of material damage. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing cutting versatility and precision.</p></p>
