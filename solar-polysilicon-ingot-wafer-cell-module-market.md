<p><p>The solar polysilicon ingot wafer cell module market features two primary connection types: series and parallel connections. In series connection, solar cells are linked end-to-end, increasing voltage and maintaining consistent current, ideal for maximizing energy output in limited space. Conversely, parallel connection links cells side-by-side, enhancing current flow while maintaining voltage, suited for larger installations where shading on individual cells can impact overall efficiency. Each method optimizes energy capture and system performance based on installation requirements.</p></p>
