<p><p>The marine control valves market encompasses various types, primarily including ball valves and butterfly valves. Ball valves are characterized by their spherical closure element, providing quick on-off flow control with minimal pressure drop. Butterfly valves, featuring a rotating disc, offer efficient regulation of flow in larger pipelines and are lightweight and compact. Other types may include gate valves, check valves, and pressure relief valves. These components are essential for managing liquid and gas flows in marine applications, ensuring operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
