<p><p>Fracking water treatment systems focus on managing the water used and produced in hydraulic fracturing. The Plug and Perf method involves using plugs to isolate sections of the well, allowing for targeted fracking, while the Sliding Sleeves technique enables selective stimulation by sliding open designed sleeves in the casing. Both methods require effective water treatment systems to ensure the produced water is properly treated for reuse or disposal, ultimately promoting environmental sustainability and resource efficiency in the fracking industry.</p></p>
