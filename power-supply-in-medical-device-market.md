<p><p>In the medical device market, power supplies are categorized primarily into AC-DC and DC-DC power supplies, along with other types. AC-DC power supplies convert alternating current from the mains into direct current, essential for devices like imaging systems and monitors. DC-DC power supplies, on the other hand, provide voltage regulation and conversion for devices such as portable diagnostic equipment. Other market types may include uninterruptible power supplies and battery solutions, ensuring reliability and safety in critical medical applications.</p></p>
