<p><strong>The Elliptical Fitness Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Front-Drive</li><li>Center-Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elliptical fitness machines are categorized based on their drive system: rear-drive, front-drive, and center-drive. Rear-drive machines have the flywheel located at the back, offering a natural stride and often a more stable feel. Front-drive models place the flywheel at the front, resulting in a more compact design and a steeper incline that can enhance workout intensity. Center-drive ellipticals feature the flywheel in the middle, providing a balanced workout experience and a more space-efficient design, ideal for smaller areas.</p></p>
