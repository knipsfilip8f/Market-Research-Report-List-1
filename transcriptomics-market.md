<p><p>The transcriptomics market encompasses various technologies for analyzing gene expression. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) enables the amplification of specific RNA sequences for detailed study. Microarrays allow for the simultaneous examination of thousands of genes, providing a broad view of expression levels across multiple samples. Sequencing, particularly RNA sequencing (RNA-seq), offers high-resolution analysis by determining the precise sequence of RNA molecules, revealing complex transcriptomes and alternative splicing events. Together, these methods drive advancements in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
