<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable telephone market can be categorized into two main types: Analog and DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications). Analog phones utilize traditional radio frequencies for communication, delivering basic voice services with limited features. In contrast, DECT phones operate on a digital platform, providing enhanced audio quality, security, longer range, and additional functionalities like voicemail and address books. DECT technology has become the preferred choice for home and business environments due to its reliability and advanced capabilities, fostering broader adoption in recent years.</p></p>
