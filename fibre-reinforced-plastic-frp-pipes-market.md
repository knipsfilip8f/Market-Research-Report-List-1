<p><p>The Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) pipes market consists of various types, including Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP), Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE), and Glass Reinforced Vinylester (GRV) pipes. GRP pipes are known for their corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, making them ideal for water and wastewater applications. GRE pipes offer superior strength and chemical resistance, suitable for oil and gas industries. GRV pipes combine attributes of both GRP and GRE, providing enhanced durability for industrial applications, especially where high-temperature resistance is crucial.</p></p>
