<p><p>The hanging gas stove market is categorized mainly into condensing and non-condensing types. Condensing gas stoves utilize advanced technology to capture and reuse exhaust gases, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing emissions. They are ideal for energy-conscious consumers and often provide optimal heating performance. In contrast, non-condensing gas stoves release exhaust gases without recovery, making them simpler and typically less expensive. However, they may result in higher energy costs and emissions, appealing to those prioritizing initial affordability over efficiency.</p></p>
