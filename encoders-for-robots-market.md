<p><strong>The Encoders for Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Relative Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encoders are vital components in robotics, serving to convert motion into signals for position or speed feedback. Absolute encoders provide precise position data by using a unique code for each angle, ensuring accurate readings even after power loss. In contrast, relative encoders, or incremental encoders, measure motion by tracking changes from a reference point, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous rotation without absolute position information. Both types are critical for enhancing robotic functionality and precision in automation systems.</p></p>
