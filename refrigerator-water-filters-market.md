<p><strong>The Refrigerator Water Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerator water filters market primarily features two types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) filters and Carbon Block filters. GAC filters utilize granules of activated carbon to effectively reduce impurities, odors, and chlorine, providing improved taste and clarity to drinking water. In contrast, Carbon Block filters offer a denser structure, enhancing contaminant removal efficiency by trapping particles more effectively. Both filter types are essential for ensuring clean, safe drinking water directly from refrigerators, catering to consumer demands for better filtration solutions.</p></p>
