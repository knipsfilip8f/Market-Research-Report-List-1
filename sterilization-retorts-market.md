<p><strong>The Sterilization Retorts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Retorts</li><li>Rotary Retorts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization retorts market includes two main types: static retorts and rotary retorts. Static retorts maintain a fixed position during the sterilization process, allowing products to be treated uniformly through steam or water circulation. They are ideal for delicate items or bulk processing. In contrast, rotary retorts utilize a rotating mechanism to enhance heat transfer and ensure even sterilization across all product surfaces, making them suitable for continuous production lines and larger batches. Both types enhance food safety and shelf life.</p></p>
