<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Pressure-sensitive</li><li>Capacitive Touch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tablet stylus and touchscreen pen market can be categorized into two main types: electromagnetic pressure-sensitive and capacitive touch. Electromagnetic pressure-sensitive styluses utilize electromagnetic fields to detect pressure levels, allowing for precise control and varied line thickness. These are often used with specific devices for drawing or detailed tasks. Capacitive touch pens, on the other hand, mimic the touch of a finger on a touchscreen, suitable for a wide range of devices, providing basic functionality for navigation and writing.</p></p>
