<p><p>An air curtain is a device that creates a barrier of air across an opening, effectively separating different environments. It is typically installed over doorways, entrances, and other access points to maintain temperature control, enhance energy efficiency, and prevent the entry of dust, insects, and pollutants. Air curtains are widely used in commercial spaces, restaurants, and retail establishments, contributing to an improved customer experience and reducing energy costs.</p><p>The Air Curtain Market is poised for growth, driven by increasing awareness of energy conservation, rising demand for energy-efficient HVAC solutions, and the need for maintaining indoor air quality. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on hygiene and health, particularly post-pandemic, is propelling the adoption of air curtains in healthcare facilities and public spaces.
