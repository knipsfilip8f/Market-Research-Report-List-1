<p><p>Modular construction in healthcare involves prefabricated structures designed for medical facilities. The two primary market types are permanent modular and relocatable modular. Permanent modular construction offers durable, long-lasting buildings that are integrated into existing healthcare systems, often used for hospitals and clinics. Relocatable modular construction provides flexible, temporary solutions that can be moved or altered as needs change, ideal for urgent care centers or additional patient space. Both types enhance efficiency, speed, and customizable design in healthcare infrastructure.</p></p>
