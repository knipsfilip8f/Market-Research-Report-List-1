<p><p>The ornamental tropical fish feed market is categorized into two primary types: animal feed and plant feed. Animal feed primarily consists of high-protein pellets, flakes, and granules made from fish meal, shrimp, and other protein sources, catering to carnivorous species. In contrast, plant feed includes algae, spirulina, and plant-based ingredients designed for herbivorous and omnivorous fish. Both types are crucial for ensuring optimal growth, health, and vibrant colors in tropical fish, catering to the diverse dietary needs of various species.</p></p>
