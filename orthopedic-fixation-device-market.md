<p><strong>The Orthopedic Fixation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixation Device</li><li>Internal Fixation Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic fixation device market encompasses two primary types: external and internal fixation devices. External fixation devices are applied externally to stabilize fractures using rods and pins that connect to the bone from outside the skin. Internal fixation devices, such as plates, screws, and intramedullary nails, are surgically implanted within the body to ensure proper alignment and healing of fractured bones. Both types play crucial roles in orthopedic surgery, catering to different fracture scenarios and patient needs for effective recovery.</p></p>
