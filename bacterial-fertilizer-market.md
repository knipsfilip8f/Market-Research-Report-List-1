<p><p>The bacterial fertilizer market consists of three primary types: nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, and others. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers contain bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form accessible to plants, enhancing growth. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers help release bound phosphates in soil, making them available for plant uptake, thus improving root development. The "others" category includes various specialized bacterial formulations that target different soil nutrient deficiencies and promote overall soil health, contributing to sustainable agricultural practices and improving yield.</p></p>
