<p><p>The Industrial Noise Control System market is divided into two main types: Internal and External Industrial Noise Control Systems. Internal Industrial Noise Control focuses on reducing noise generated within the facility through the use of sound-absorbing materials, acoustic panels, and machinery enclosures. In contrast, External Industrial Noise Control Systems are designed to mitigate noise pollution impacting the surrounding environment, utilizing sound barriers, shields, and zoning regulations. Both systems aim to improve workplace safety and comply with environmental noise standards.</p></p>
