<p><p>The mechanical control valves market is divided into four main types. Manual control valves are operated by hand, allowing precise flow regulation. Pneumatic control valves utilize compressed air for actuation, offering quick response times and reliability. Hydraulic control valves are powered by hydraulic fluid, providing strong force and efficient operation in heavy-duty applications. Electric control valves use electrical signals for automatic operation, enabling advanced control and integration with digital systems. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
