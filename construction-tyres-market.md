<p><strong>The Construction Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction tires market is primarily divided into two types: bias ply and radial ply tires. Bias ply tires feature layers of fabric that crisscross, providing durability and flexibility, making them suitable for rough terrains and load-bearing applications. On the other hand, radial ply tires have plies arranged perpendicular to the tread, enhancing traction, stability, and fuel efficiency. Radial tires are gaining popularity due to their superior performance and longevity, while bias ply tires remain favored for specific heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
