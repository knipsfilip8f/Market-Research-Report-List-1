<p><strong>The Polyurethane Grouts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne Polyurethane Grouts</li><li>Solventborne Polyurethane Grouts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane grouts market comprises two main types: waterborne and solventborne polyurethane grouts. Waterborne polyurethane grouts utilize water as a primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and enhanced environmental safety, making them popular in eco-conscious applications. In contrast, solventborne polyurethane grouts contain organic solvents, providing superior adhesion and flexibility in harsher conditions, but may pose higher environmental and health concerns due to elevated VOC levels. Both types are widely used in construction, waterproofing, and industrial applications.</p></p>
