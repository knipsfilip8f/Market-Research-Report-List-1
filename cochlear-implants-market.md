<p><ul><li>Unilateral</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cochlear implants market can be categorized into unilateral and binaural types. Unilateral implants target individuals with hearing loss in one ear, enhancing sound perception and communication abilities. Binaural implants, on the other hand, are designed for patients with hearing impairment in both ears, providing better spatial awareness and improved sound localization. The choice between these options depends on the severity of hearing loss, patient preference, and the desired auditory experience, influencing market trends and growth.</p></p>
