<p><p>The desalination valves market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. Butterfly valves are widely used for on-off and throttling applications due to their space efficiency. Diaphragm valves offer excellent sealing and are ideal for corrosive fluids. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional flow in desalination processes. Ball valves provide reliable shutdown and flow control with minimal pressure drop. Other types include gate, globe, and solenoid valves, catering to distinct operational needs in desalination systems.</p></p>
