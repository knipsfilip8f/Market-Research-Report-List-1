<p><p>Circulating fluidized bed (CFB) boilers operate using fine particles to enhance heat transfer and combustion efficiency. The market can be segmented into three main types: subcritical, supercritical, and ultra-supercritical. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below the critical pressure, whereas supercritical models function above this threshold, allowing for higher efficiency and reduced emissions. Ultra-supercritical boilers further optimize performance by maximizing temperature and pressure, resulting in even greater efficiency and lower fuel consumption, making them suitable for modern, eco-friendly energy production.</p></p>
