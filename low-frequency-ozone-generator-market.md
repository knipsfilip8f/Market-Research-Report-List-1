Water cooling ozone generators utilize a liquid cooling mechanism to dissipate heat, ensuring efficient operation during prolonged use. They are typically employed in industrial applications where high ozone output is required. In contrast, wind cooling ozone generators rely on airflow for cooling, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations or environments where space and mobility are crucial.
