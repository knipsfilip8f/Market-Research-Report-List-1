<p><strong>The Penicillin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aminopenicillins</li><li>Antipseudomonal Penicillins</li><li>Beta-lactamase Inhibitors</li><li>Natural Penicillins</li><li>Penicillinase Resistant Penicillins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The penicillin market is categorized into several types based on their chemical structure and use. Aminopenicillins, such as ampicillin and amoxicillin, target a broad range of bacteria. Antipseudomonal penicillins, like piperacillin, specifically combat Pseudomonas infections. Beta-lactamase inhibitors enhance the effectiveness of penicillins against resistant bacteria. Natural penicillins, such as penicillin G, are effective against specific organisms. Lastly, penicillinase-resistant penicillins, like methicillin, are designed to withstand bacterial resistance mechanisms, ensuring effectiveness against certain resistant strains.</p></p>
