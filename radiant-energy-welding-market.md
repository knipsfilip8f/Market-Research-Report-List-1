<p><strong>The Radiant Energy Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Beam Welding</li><li>Electron Beam Welding (EBW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiant Energy Welding market comprises primarily two advanced techniques: Laser Beam Welding (LBW) and Electron Beam Welding (EBW). LBW utilizes focused laser beams to melt and join materials, offering precision and minimal thermal distortion, making it ideal for thin-walled structures and intricate designs. In contrast, EBW employs a concentrated beam of high-energy electrons, allowing for deep penetration and welding of thick materials in a vacuum, which enhances quality and reduces contamination. Both methods are increasingly adopted across various industries for their efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
