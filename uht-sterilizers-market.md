<p><strong>The UHT Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating</li><li>Indirect Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UHT sterilizers market is primarily segmented into two types: direct heating and indirect heating. Direct heating involves rapidly heating the product using steam or hot air, allowing for instant sterilization. This method is efficient and preserves the product's flavor and nutrients. In contrast, indirect heating employs heat exchangers to elevate temperature, minimizing product contact with heat sources. This technique is gentler on sensitive products, maintaining quality while ensuring effective sterilization. Both methods are crucial for safe food and beverage processing.</p></p>
