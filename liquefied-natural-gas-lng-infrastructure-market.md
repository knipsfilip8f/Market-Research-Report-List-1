<p><ul><li>Liquefaction Terminal</li><li>Regasification Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) infrastructure market encompasses two primary components: liquefaction terminals and regasification terminals. Liquefaction terminals convert natural gas into LNG by cooling it to a liquid state for efficient transport and storage. Conversely, regasification terminals convert LNG back into gaseous form upon arrival at its destination, enabling distribution through pipelines. Both terminal types are essential for global LNG supply chains, facilitating trade and ensuring energy security across various markets.</p></p>
