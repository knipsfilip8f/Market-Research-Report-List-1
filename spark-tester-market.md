<p><p>The Spark Tester market is divided into two main types based on frequency: Mains Frequency Spark Tester and High Frequency Spark Tester. Mains Frequency Spark Testers operate at the standard power line frequency, typically used for routine electrical testing, ensuring insulation integrity and safety. In contrast, High Frequency Spark Testers generate higher frequency signals, which are effective in detecting minute insulation defects and are often utilized in specialized applications like aerospace or high-voltage equipment testing, enhancing reliability and performance validation.</p></p>
