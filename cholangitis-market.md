<p><p>The cholangitis market is segmented into several types based on the underlying conditions. Acute cholangitis, often caused by bile duct obstruction, presents sudden symptoms and requires urgent treatment. Primary sclerosing cholangitis is a chronic inflammatory condition leading to bile duct scarring and liver damage, often linked to autoimmune diseases. Secondary sclerosing cholangitis results from external factors like infections or disease processes obstructing the bile duct. Recurrent pyogenic cholangitis, typically prevalent in certain populations, involves recurrent infections due to bile duct abnormalities.</p></p>
