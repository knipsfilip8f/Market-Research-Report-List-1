<p><p>The rotating rack oven market comprises primarily two types: electric deck ovens and gas deck ovens. Electric deck ovens utilize electric heating elements to provide consistent and efficient baking, ideal for smaller establishments or artisan bakeries. In contrast, gas deck ovens harness gas burners for heat, offering faster recovery times and often lower operating costs, making them popular in larger commercial kitchens. Both types deliver uniform baking results, but the choice depends on factors like energy source availability, operational needs, and cost efficiency.</p></p>
