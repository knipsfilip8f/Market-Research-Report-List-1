<p><strong>The Semiconductor Testing Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ProbeCard</li><li>LoadBoard</li><li>Burn-inBoard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor testing board market encompasses three main types: Probe Cards, Load Boards, and Burn-in Boards. Probe Cards are used for electrically testing semiconductor wafers by facilitating connections between test equipment and the device. Load Boards are employed during the final testing phase, enabling the application of signals and power to the semiconductor devices within their packaging. Burn-in Boards are designed to stress-test semiconductor devices under extreme conditions to identify early failures, ensuring reliability before market release.</p></p>
